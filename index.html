Luke Rogers - About Me

Bio

I was born in 1998 in Lake Forest, Illinois and have lived in Colorado, Georgia, and Boston. My family lives in Seattle. I don't have any pets but some day I hope to have a dog named Duke.

Education

I went to highschool in Boston at Boston College High School. After that, I attended and recieved my Bachelors degree in business at Wheaton College. I am now a student at Code Fellows in the 201 class.

Job Experience

My highschool summer jobs included construction manual laborer and park maintenance worker. During college I had a variety of summer jobs including summer camp counselor, mover, and property management intern. After college, I took a job as a project manager at a small digital experience company and worked with a number of developers. After two years, I was laid off due to financial struggles. I am now a part time Team Member in Whole Food's butcher shop and a full-time Code Fellows student.

Goals

My goal is to become a team leader of development at a large tech company such as Microsoft or Google. I also desire to thrive financially.
